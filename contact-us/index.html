Account & data deletion

If you created an account in any app published by Jinacode Systems, you can request that your account and its associated data be permanently deleted at any time. This applies to every app we publish, on any platform.

To request deletion:

  1. Email developers@jinacode.systems with the subject Delete my account.
  2. Include the app name and the email address or username your account is registered under (send the request from that email if possible, so we can verify it's you).
  3. We confirm the request and permanently delete your account within 30 days.

What gets deleted: your account profile (name, email, username), your activity and usage history, content you created in the app, and any other data linked to your account.

What may be retained: records we are legally required to keep (for example, for accounting, fraud prevention, or dispute resolution) are retained only as long as the law requires, then deleted. Anonymized, aggregated statistics that can no longer identify you may also be retained.

You can also request a copy of your data, or correction of inaccurate data, through the same email address.
